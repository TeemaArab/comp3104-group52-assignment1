George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology located in Toronto, 
Ontario, Canada. It was established in 1967 and is named after George Brown, 
one of the Fathers of Confederation.

#The second Commits

The college offers over 160 programs, including diploma, degree, and certificate programs 
in areas such as business, technology, arts, and health sciences. 
It has a strong focus on experiential learning, with many programs offering field placements, 
internships, and industry partnerships.
