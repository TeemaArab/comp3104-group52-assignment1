George Brown College (GBC) is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Established in 1967, GBC offers a wide variety of full-time and part-time programs, including diplomas, certificates, and degrees in fields such as business, health sciences, design, hospitality, construction, and technology.

The college is known for its strong connections with industry and focuses on providing hands-on learning experiences to prepare students for their careers. With campuses across Toronto, GBC attracts a diverse student population and has a reputation for fostering innovation and practical skills.
